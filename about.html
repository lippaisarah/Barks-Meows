ABOUT ME

There are few things Hannah Parry loves more than artisanal avocado toast and a perfectly sunny day in Los Angeles. If you miss her at the local brunch hot spot, you can find her leading the Performance Creative department at Wpromote to ensure the team delivers best-in-class creative across all paid channels and client verticals.

With the heart of a graphic designer and the mind of a growth marketer, Hannah has worked in the digital marketing and design industries on both the agency and brand side. Hannah has built out digital marketing channels from the ground up for a D2C beauty startup, managed multi- million dollar paid social budgets for a large D2C apparel brand, and worked professionally as a graphic designer.
